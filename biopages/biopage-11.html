Rickard Berg

Rickard is a seasoned Polar Expedition leader and Musher from Sweden. He is also a ships officer and professional wilderness guide who has spent the last decade working with expeditions both land-based with dogsledding and on the ocean on ships or kayaks in Norway, Svalbard, Greenland, Iceland, The Falkland Islands, South Gerogia and Antarctica. He also spent two seasons as a dogsled instructor for the British Army in their Winter adventure training base in Canada's Rocky Mountains. Currently he works full time as a Swedish Coast Guard.
